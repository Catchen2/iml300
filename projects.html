Project 2: HyperNarrative

In this project, I wanted to explore what home means to me. I narrowed it down to 3 things that feel the most like home: people, food, and places. I used old pictures to show some of my core memories of those things. I mainly used pictures from China because I miss that home and my grandparents a lot and I still can't travel back there to see them, so this website lets me travel back in time and remember the good times, places, and people in Beijing! I also included a page where you can draw your own home.

Final: Home Is

Project 3: Networked Justice

This project is about corruption in America's pharmaceutical industry that affects everyday Americans who rely on medication to survive and live with chronic illnesses. As the issue becomes more apparent to the public, more websites, companies, and government initiatives, like the Insulin Act, are aiming to help make medicine more affordable for everyone.

Final: Big Pharma
